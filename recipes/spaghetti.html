Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This Classic Spaghetti with Marinara Sauce recipe is a timeless Italian favorite, featuring a rich and flavorful tomato sauce made from sautéed onions, garlic, and aromatic herbs. Topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh basil, this meal is both simple to prepare and delightfully delicious, making it a perfect choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Salt (for the pasta water)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, and sugar (if using).
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  8. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  9. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water and then drain the spaghetti.
  10. Add the cooked spaghetti to the saucepan with the marinara sauce.
  11. Toss to coat the pasta evenly, adding a bit of the reserved pasta water if needed to reach the desired consistency.
  12. Serve the spaghetti topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
