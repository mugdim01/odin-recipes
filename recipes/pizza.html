Pizza Recipe

Description

This Homemade Margherita Pizza recipe delivers a classic and delicious pizza experience with a perfectly crisp crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and fresh mozzarella. Topped with fragrant basil leaves and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, this pizza is a simple yet satisfying dish that captures the essence of Italian cuisine. Perfect for any pizza night, this recipe brings the taste of an authentic pizzeria right into your home.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
